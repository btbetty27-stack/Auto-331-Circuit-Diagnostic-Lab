🔍 DIAGNOSTICS TRAINER

Each circuit has been wired correctly but contains a single fault. Your job is to use the DVOM to diagnose the fault and identify its location.

Meter Modes: DC Voltage measures voltage in a live circuit. Resistance measures resistance with the circuit off. Continuity beeps when a path is complete. Use the mode buttons or click the dial.

Test Points: Click any yellow dot on the circuit to place a probe. Select RED or BLACK probe first, then click the test point.

Diagnosing: Click any component or wire segment on the circuit to select it as the fault location. Then click Submit Diagnosis.

Attempts: You have 3 attempts per circuit. A hint is revealed after each incorrect attempt.
